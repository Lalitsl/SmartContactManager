Smart Contact Manager About Page

What is contact management - Salesforce Contact management is the process of recording contacts' details and tracking their interactions with a business. Such systems have gradually evolved into an aspect of customer relationship management (CRM) systems, which allow businesses to improve sales and service levels leveraging a wider range of data.
The "SmartContactManager" project is a helpful tool for managing users and their contacts. It allows users to create accounts, log in, and handle forgotten passwords securely. For managing contacts, it enables adding, editing, and deleting contacts, along with uploading images and finding contacts quickly. Moreover, it supports breaking down contacts into manageable chunks and handling payments. Additionally, it includes some extra features like chatting, exporting and importing contacts, sorting contacts into groups, and integrating with emails and calendars. This project serves as an excellent learning tool for anyone interested in Java programming and application development.
